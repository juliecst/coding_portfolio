Julie C. Stamm

I am a dancer with a background in neuroaesthetics. My current practice invests in how these backgrounds can meet. I am interested in a sense of unity between a piece of work and the audience. I hope for movement to not stop at my skin which made me explore different (technology based) media. My work is driven by wonderings about the space between us and crossings between transitory waiting rooms. As a member of dajci*collective we experiment with nontraditional performative spaces and a commitment to fragile awkwardness.
